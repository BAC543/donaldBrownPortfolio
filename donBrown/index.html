Donald Brown For Judge of Poughkeepsie "A court system which has functioned on autopilot for too long is collapsing under its own weight in the face of an unprecedented drug epidemic, pervasive mental illness and costly and crippling incarceration rates. The failure of the courts to adapt to these changes is reaching crisis stage. My experience makes me uniquely qualified to bring a fresh but seasoned response to these challenges."
